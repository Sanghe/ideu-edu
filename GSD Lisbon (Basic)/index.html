about:

This intensive course fosters knowledge acquisition or deepening and increases the degree of clinical experience, allowing participants to gain confidence and embrace excellence in implantology supported by the most advanced technical and scientific standard, with visible results in their clinical practice. For more experienced participants this is an opportunity to recycle previous knowledge and acquire greater know-how, through the intense work with patients. By the end of the course, participants will be able to draw-up individual treatment plans, identify the different options and introduce dental implants in their clinical practice, from implant placement surgery to the implant supported prosthetic rehabilitation.
